Spicy Tomato Pasta

Ingredients
  • 2 T. Olive Oil
  • 1 T. Red Pepper Flakes
  • 3 Little Slivers Anchovie
  • 4 Cloves Garlic
  • 2 T. Capers
  • 2 cans Diced Tomato
  • 12 oz. Can of Tuna
  • 14.5 oz. Pasta
Directions
  1. Cook on medium low starting with top of list and moving down.
  2. Cook pasta
  3. Toss Together
