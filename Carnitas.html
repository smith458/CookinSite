Carnitas

Ingredients
  • 3-4 lbs Pork Butt
  • 1 Onion
  • 4 Cloves Garlic
  • 2 Oranges
  • 1 T Salt
  • 1 T Cumin
  • 1 1/2 t Oregano
  • 1 1/2 t Coriander
  • 1/2 t Cinamon
  • 4 bay leaves
  • 1/4 t Red Pepper Flakes
  • 1 Cup Broth
Directions
  1. Combine all ingredients in crockpot and cook for 11 hours.
  2. Shred and broil for 5-10 minutes until crispy.
  3. Pour some juice from crockpot over top.
