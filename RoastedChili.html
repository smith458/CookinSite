Roasted Chili

Ingredients
  • 2 lbs Ground Beef
  • 3/4 t Baking Soda
  • 5 Poblano Chilies
  • 4 Anaheim Peppers
  • 1 Onion
  • 8 cloves Garlic
  • 1 can Chicken Broth
  • 2 T Cumin
  • 1 T Coriander
  • 1 T Paprika
  • 1 T Garlic Powder
  • 2 t Oregano
  • 1 can Crushed Tomatoes
  • 1 can Diced Tomatoes
  • 1 can Pinto Beans
  • 1 T. Vinegar
Directions
  1. Toss beef with 1 tablespoon water, 3/4 teaspoon salt, and baking soda in bowl until thoroughly combined. Set aside for 20 minutes.
  2. Preheat oven to 275.
  3. Broil peppers, onion, and garlic until blackened (about 10-20 minutes).
  4. Peel and chop peppers. Toss the skins and seeds in chicken broth.
  5. Blend peppers, garlic, and broth.
  6. Over medium heat, heat oil until shimmering. Cook onion for a minute.
  7. Toss the ground beef into the pan and cook until browned (12-15 mins).
  8. Add in pepper mixture and allow to cook for a couple minutes.
  9. Add in tomatoes. Scrape up fond and add beans.
  10. Cover and put in oven for 1.5 to 2 hours.
  11. Add vinegar before serving.
Notes for next time:

  • Not enough chili taste (no chili powder)
  • More Beans
