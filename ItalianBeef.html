Italian Beef

Ingredients
  • 2-3 pounds Chuck Roast
  • 1 packet Italian Dressing Seasoning
  • 1 packet Aus Jus Seasoning
  • 1-2 16 oz. jars Pepperoncini (sliced)
  • Rolls
  • Cheese
Directions
  1. Toss all ingredients in a slow cooker and set on low for 8 hours
  2. Shred and add to rolls with cheese
