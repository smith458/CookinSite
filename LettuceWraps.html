Roasted Chili

Ingredients
  • 1 T Sesame Oil
  • 1 lb Ground Chicken
  • 1 can Water Chestnuts
  • 2 cloves Garlic
  • 8 oz White Mushrooms
  • 1/4 c Hoisin Sauce
  • 1/4 c Soy Sauce
  • 1 T Rice Wine Vinegar
  • 1 T Ginger
  • 1 T Sirarcha
  • 2 Green Onions
  • 1 Head Lettuce
  • Roasted Peanuts, Chopped
Directions
  1. Cook Chickem
  2. Add onion, garlic, mushrooms.
  3. Add Sauce
  4. Add chestnuts
